The AI Inflection Point

Why Now

AI transformation is happening in two distinct waves. Organizations that understand the difference will have a significant advantage.

2025

The Year of Building

Tools like Replit let non-developers build functional products for the first time. We used these tools to create the Sanibel Recovery Dashboard — proving what’s possible.

Example

“Build me a sales tracking spreadsheet.”

AI creates it. You use it.

2026

The Year of Workflows

AI is no longer just building things. It’s working alongside people continuously — simplifying legacy systems, automating multi-step workflows, redesigning processes.

Example

“Help me redesign how we manage sales.”

AI works with you daily, adapting as needs change.

The shift: from “AI as a tool that produces outputs” to “AI as a collaborator that transforms how work gets done.”
